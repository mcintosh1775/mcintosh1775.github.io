Fedimints could be a way to continue to scale Bitcoin to meet the needs of the world. The reality is that not everyone will be able to self-custody. For those who can not - what are the solutions? While Fedimints are not fully developed but they show great promise as a way to help meet the needs of a population that is unplugging from the fiat system. Let's discuss.
